Info

Welcome to Mastermind, in this game the computer has generated a 4 color pattern that you have to guess in the amount of turns given.

Once you have made your first guess the pegs in the right hand box should fill with one of these 3 colors:

  • Gold Peg: You have an exact match (correct color and postion)
  • White w/ Green Outline: You have the one correct color
  • Stay Dark: Your guess is 100% wrong. Yikes.

Win: You guess the correct parttern before the turn limit.

Lose: You hit the guess limit before being able to guess the pattern correctly.

You must select 4 choices to make a guess!
